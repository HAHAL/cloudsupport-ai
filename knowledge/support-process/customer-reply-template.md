# Customer Reply Template

## Scenario

适用于技术支持人员在证据不足或初步定位阶段生成专业、克制、可发送给客户的回复草稿。

## Template

Dear Customer,

Thank you for contacting us.

Based on the current information, the issue may be related to the reported error code, permission configuration, request parameters, service latency, or upstream dependency behavior. We are not making a final conclusion yet and will continue the investigation once the required evidence is available.

Could you please provide the following information:

1. Request ID or trace ID
2. Timestamp with timezone
3. Affected account, tenant, environment, or workspace
4. Full error response with sensitive values removed
5. Reproduction steps and business impact

Once we receive the above information, we will continue the analysis and provide the next update.

Best regards,
Technical Support Team

## Notes

- 不要在证据不足时直接确认根因。
- 明确下一步需要客户补充的信息。
- 回复语气保持专业、克制和可执行。
